Koho Busines Cards

Team Koho gets Koho business cards.

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux at 2016-05-02

Scott Bell designed the Koho business cards to look like credit cards — I thought that was both fitting and clever 🙂

They are even made out of plastic (rather than some type of paper, like most business cards).

I have had many business cards over the decades — but this is the first plastic business card I have had.
